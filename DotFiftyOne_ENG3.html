On Thursday, July 18th at 7:00 p.m., Dot Fiftyone Gallery presents the book launch of artist María Boneo in Miami. The artist will engage in a conversation about her recent work and current production with recognized art critics Ana María Battistozzi and Julio Sánchez, art book designer Manuela López Anaya, cultural producer Carolina Bach, and sculptor Luciano Garbati.

María Boneo was born in Belgrade, Yugoslavia, and lives in Argentina since 1968. Boneo studied at the Escuela Nacional de Bellas Artes and in various artist’s workshops. Boneo participated in numerous solo and group shows, including institutions such as the Museo de Arte Decorativo, Museo Quinquela Martín, GC Arte, Galería Del InfinitoArte, Studio Abba, in Florence, and María Calcaterra Galería, in Argentina. She was selected to be part of various salons and awards and received a distinction at the Salón Nacional de Artes Visuales at the Paláis de Glace, in Buenos Aires.

The event will be delivered in Spanish. A reception will follow the conversation.
